Apex Predators

Ghost Mantis

Phidippus audax — big expressive eyes, iridescent green fangs, hunts by stalking. Lives ~1 year.

APEX PREDATOR

Pseudoscorpion

Tiny scorpion-like predator with pincers. Breeds in terrarium — permanent self-sustaining apex.

BACKUP APEX
Land Crew

Dairy Cow Isopods

Porcellio laevis "Dairy Cow" — black & white spotted like tiny cows. Decomposers that break down leaf litter.

LAND CREW

Springtails

Essential cleanup crew. Eat mold, dead matter, and waste. The foundation of the land ecosystem.

LAND CREW

Flightless Fruit Flies

Drosophila melanogaster — breed on decaying matter. Key prey species for the jumping spider.

PREY

Small Land Snails

Graze on algae and decaying plant matter. Charming and self-sustaining.

LAND CREW
Water Animals

Opae Ula Shrimp

Halocaridina rubra — bright red Hawaiian shrimp. Lives 20+ years in sealed systems. Zero feeding needed.

WATER STAR

Assassin Snail

Clea helena — yellow & brown striped shell. Hunts other snails, keeps populations balanced.

WATER PREDATOR

Ramshorn Snail

Algae-eating snail with spiral shell. Breeds readily — serves as food for assassin snails.

WATER CREW

Daphnia (Water Fleas)

Tiny translucent crustaceans that pulse through water in mesmerizing swarms. Feed on algae.

WATER CREW

Scuds (Amphipods)

Fast-darting shrimp-like crustaceans. Active detritivores that keep the water section clean.

WATER CREW

Ostracods (Seed Shrimp)

Microscopic crustaceans in tiny shells. Add biodiversity to the water column.

WATER CREW

Bladder Snails

Small translucent snails that eat detritus. Breed quickly — food source for assassin snails.

WATER CREW
Land Plants

Jewel Orchid

Macodes petola — velvety dark leaves with glowing gold lightning-bolt veins. The showpiece plant.

LAND PLANT

Peacock Begonia

Begonia pavonina — iridescent blue-green shimmer under low light. Truly stunning.

LAND PLANT

Fittonia (Nerve Plant)

Pink and white vein varieties. Near-neon colors under grow lights.

LAND PLANT

Ruby Red Selaginella

S. erythropus — deep red undersides, much more striking than plain green spikemoss.

LAND PLANT

Mini Bromeliad

Neoregelia — bright reds and pinks. Holds tiny water pools in its center.

LAND PLANT

Marcgravia

Shingling vine with flat leaves that hug surfaces. Creates a dramatic living wall effect.

LAND PLANT

Mood Moss

Dicranum — fluffy mounded moss with lush texture. Creates a miniature forest floor.

LAND PLANT
Water Plants

Bucephalandra

Metallic sheen in reds, blues, greens. Attaches to rocks in the water section.

WATER PLANT

Rotala rotundifolia

Turns pink and red under good light. Adds beautiful color to the water section.

WATER PLANT

Marimo Moss Balls

Perfectly round, velvety green algae balls. Everyone's favorite.

WATER PLANT

Java Moss

Taxiphyllum barbieri — feathery aquatic moss. Grows on rocks and driftwood.
